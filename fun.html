What I like to do with my free time

Farmer's Market

The farmer's market in Madison in the fall and spring is so much fun. Me and my friends go almost every saturday it is open and we try different types of cheeses, breads, and desserts.

Sports Games

One of the best parts of being at a Big 10 school is going to the sports games. I have been to the hockey, basketball, and football games several times. I am so glad they opened fans to the public this year after Covid.

Spending time Outside

The lakeshore path is a scenic route in Madison that my friends and I love to walk for fun, especially when the weather is nice. In the winter, when it is not too cold outside, me and my friends walk on the frozen lake.

Chi Omega Sorority

Freshman year, I joined Chi Omega, a sorority. I have meet so many new friends and have made so many memories while being apart of this group. We do philanthropy work with the Make a Wish foundation which is always so rewarding and fun.
